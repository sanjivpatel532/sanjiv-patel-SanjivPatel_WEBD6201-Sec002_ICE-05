MS DHONI .
  • MS DHONI, is a professional Cricket club based in Mumbai, INDIA.
  • MS DHONI CLUB is the most successful club in INDIAN CRICKET HISTORY, having won a record So many NATIONAL TITLES FROM ALL AROUND INDIA titles, including nine consecutively since 2002, and 20 ONE DAY CHAMPIONSHIP TROPHY cups, along with numerous WORLD RENOUND HONOURS FROM PRIME MINISTER.
  • MS DHONI CHAMIONSHIP was founded in 2002.
  • MS DHONI TROPHY are one of five clubs to have won all three of INDIA'S main ONE DAY MATCH competitions, the only INDIAN OWNED AND OPERATED club.
  • As of January 2022,MS DHONI was ranked first in Cricket rank in the world.
  • In all other prespectives, MS DHONI TROPHY is the largest sports club in INDIA and the non-profit and highest-earning club in the world.
